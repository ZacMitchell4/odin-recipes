Lasagna

Description

This cheesy lasagna will delight your family both young and old. Easy to make on a busy night and perfect for leftover lunch or dinner the next day.

Ingredients

  • 200ml Bechemel Sauce
  • 5 sheets of pasta sheets
  • 300ml tomato paste
  • 200g mozarella cheese
  • parsely to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees celcius
  2. Cook mince for 10 mins on medium heat or until browned
  3. Place 1 sheet of pasta
  4. Pour 1 layer of tomato paste
  5. Spread 1 layer of mince
  6. Spread 1 layer of bechemel sauce
  7. Repeat steps 2-4 until 5 layers are complete
  8. Sprinkle Mozarella cheese over the top
  9. Place in oven and cook for 45 minutes
