Poached Eggs

Description

Perfect for any breakfast, getting the eggs just right takes a few practice runs, but after a few times you'll be poaching the best eggs this side of the mississippi.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • white vinegar
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. crack 2 eggs into 2 small bowls
  2. fill a small saucepan with water and pour in a splash of white vinegar (to help keep the egg whites together)
  3. once boiled, stir the water with a whisk, creating a vortex
  4. pour the 2 eggs from the bowls into the vortex (the vortex swirls the egg whites around the yolk)
  5. pop your toast into the toaster until ready
  6. lift one egg out of water with a ladle and wobble it to see if the egg white is jiggly, but slightly firm
  7. if slightly firm, take out and place on paper towels to drain. If still runny, place back in for another 30 seconds or so
  8. once dry, place eggs on toast and season with salt and pepper
