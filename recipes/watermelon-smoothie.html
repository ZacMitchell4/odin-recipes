Watermelon Smoothie

Description

For those summer days by the pool with the breeze brushing your cheeks as you float around the pool. This smoothie is a smash hit with people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole watermelon
  • 300ml coconut water
  • 400ml almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 5ml vanilla extract

Steps

  1. cut out 2 small triangles of watermelon for final display
  2. scoop out remainder of watermelon and place in blender
  3. place in coconut water, almond milk and vanilla extract
  4. blend all ingredients until mixture is smooth
  5. pour into glasses
  6. sprinkle tiny amounts of chilli powder onto watermelon triangles
  7. slice a tiny cut into watermelon and place them on glass lip
